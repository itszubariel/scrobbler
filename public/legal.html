Privacy Policy

1. What We Collect

Scrobbler collects and stores only the minimum data required to function:

  • Discord User ID — to identify you across servers
  • Last.fm username — to fetch your listening data from Last.fm
  • Discord Server ID — to power server-specific features like /whoknows
  • Cached statistics — temporary caches of Last.fm data to reduce API calls (e.g. Wrapped data)

2. What We Do Not Collect

We do not read, store, or process:

  • The content of any Discord messages
  • Your Discord username, avatar, or profile information
  • Any data beyond what is explicitly listed above
  • Payment information of any kind

3. How We Use Your Data

Your Discord User ID and Last.fm username are used solely to retrieve your listening data from Last.fm and display it in response to slash commands. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or analytics.

4. Data Retention

Your linked account data is retained until you run /unlink, which permanently removes your Discord User ID and Last.fm username from our database. Cached data is automatically purged on a rolling basis.

5. Third-Party Services

Scrobbler fetches data from the Last.fm API. The data returned is governed by Last.fm's own privacy policy. We do not control what Last.fm collects about your listening activity.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect stored data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by running /unlink in any server with Scrobbler, or by contacting us via GitHub. We will process deletion requests promptly.
