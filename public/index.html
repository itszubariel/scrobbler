FAQ

Common questions

scrobbler is a Discord bot that connects to Last.fm to bring social music features to your server. Track what you're listening to, compare taste with friends, see who knows an artist best, and more, all through slash commands.
Add scrobbler to your server using the "Add to Discord" button, then run /link and enter your Last.fm username. That's it, all commands are immediately available.
Yes, completely free. All 20+ commands are available to every user with no paywalls, premium tiers, or usage limits.
Last.fm is a music tracking service that records ("scrobbles") every song you listen to across Spotify, Apple Music, and more. scrobbler uses your Last.fm data to power all its commands. You'll need a free Last.fm account to use scrobbler.
Yes. Your Last.fm account is linked to your Discord account, so your data follows you across every server scrobbler is in. Server-specific features like /whoknows reflect each server's members independently.
Open an issue on our GitHub or check the status page if something seems broken. We aim to respond quickly.
